About Me

I am a Mechanical Engineering student focused on product design and manufacturing, with a strong interest in integrating coding into real-world solutions. I am currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering and expect to graduate in May 2027.

My experience includes CAD modeling, data analysis, and hands-on mechanical work. I enjoy solving problems that require both technical precision and creative thinking. This interest began at a young age through building with LEGO and Lincoln Logs, and later developed through working on various single-piston engine platforms.

Outside of engineering, I run a photography business where I capture graduations, proposals, and other meaningful moments. This allows me to combine creativity with technical skill in a different but equally rewarding way.
